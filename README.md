## The Story

Every digital agency runs the same hamster wheel. Emails scattered across four inboxes. Client tasks tracked in spreadsheets. Social content drafted in Google Docs, posted manually, forgotten by next week. We know because we lived it.

**ConvergioAI was born inside [InstinctBits Labs](https://github.com/InstinctBits)** — the R&D arm of [DigiTech Nomads](https://digitechnomads.com), a business consultancy and digital marketing firm based in India. We built it to solve our own problems first: managing client communication across Hello, Partners, Info, and Support inboxes, auto-responding with context-aware AI, creating social content at scale, and keeping every client interaction in one place.

After months of internal use — handling real client emails, generating real social posts, tracking real tasks — we decided to open-source the core platform. Not as a marketing stunt, but because the problem we solved is universal. Every agency, every consultancy, every small business juggling emails and content deserves a tool that actually works. **ConvergioAI is that tool, and now it belongs to the community.**

## What is ConvergioAI?

ConvergioAI is an open-source, AI-powered omnichannel CRM platform designed for agencies, consultancies, and small businesses. It brings email management, AI automation, content creation, and client operations into a single unified dashboard.

### Core Capabilities

- **Unified Inbox** — Aggregate multiple email inboxes (sales, support, partnerships, info) with automatic smart tagging
- **AI Email Agent** — Context-aware auto-responder powered by Claude AI that drafts and sends replies using your company knowledge base
- **ContentBoost Engine** — Generate long posts, short posts, carousels, and short videos from a single topic with AI assistance
- **Task Management** — Convert emails into actionable tasks with priority levels, status tracking, and tag-based filtering
- **Brand Identity Management** — Store and manage multiple brand profiles with logos, voice guidelines, and target audiences
- **Knowledge Base System** — Feed your company info, services, and policies to the AI so every response sounds like you

## Roadmap

ConvergioAI follows a phased release model. Features are battle-tested internally before being published to this repository.

| Phase | What's Included | Status |
|:------|:----------------|:------:|
| **Phase 1** | Core CRM — Dashboard, Email Inbox, Task Management | Coming Soon |
| **Phase 2** | AI Email Agent — Auto-responder, Smart Tagging, Knowledge Base | Coming Soon |
| **Phase 3** | Authentication — Email/password auth, session management, protected routes | Coming Soon |
| **Phase 4** | ContentBoost — AI-powered content creation (Long Post, Short Post, Carousel, Video) | Coming Soon |
| **Phase 5** | Integrations — Cal.com booking, webhooks, third-party connectors | Coming Soon |
| **Phase 6** | Multi-tenant — Team workspaces, role-based access, shared brands | Planned |

> Want a specific feature sooner? [Open a discussion](https://github.com/InstinctBits/convergioai/discussions) and let us know.
